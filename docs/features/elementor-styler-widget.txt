Elementor Styler Widget
=======================

What it is
  An Elementor widget that lets you place and style Contact Form 7 forms inside Elementor.

How to use
  1. Edit a page with Elementor.
  2. Search for "CF7 Mate" or "CF7 Styler" in the widget panel and drag it onto your page.
  3. In the widget settings, choose your Contact Form 7 form from the dropdown.
  4. Use the Content and Style tabs to customize fields, labels, buttons, spacing, colors, and typography.
  5. Save and preview the page. The styled form appears where you placed the widget.

You need Contact Form 7 and Elementor installed and active.
