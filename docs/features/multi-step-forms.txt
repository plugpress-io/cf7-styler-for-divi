Multi Step Forms (Pro)
======================

What it is
  Splits one form into several steps (screens). Users click Next/Back. A progress indicator can show how far they are. All data is submitted together at the end.

How to use
  1. Enable "Multi Step Forms" in CF7 Mate â†’ Modules (Pro).
  2. In your Contact Form 7 form, add the step tags (e.g. [step 1], [step 2]) where each step should start. Use the CF7 Mate tag generator or shortcode as documented for multi-step.
  3. Save the form.
  4. Add the form to a page with the CF7 Styler module. The module will show one step at a time with Next/Back; you can style the progress indicator in the module settings.

Best for long forms (e.g. surveys, applications) to reduce overwhelm and increase completion.
