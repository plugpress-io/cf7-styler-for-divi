Bricks Styler Element
=====================

What it is
  A Bricks Builder element that lets you place and style Contact Form 7 forms inside Bricks.

How to use
  1. Edit a page with Bricks Builder.
  2. Search for "CF7 Mate" or "CF7 Styler" in the element panel and add it.
  3. In the element settings, choose your Contact Form 7 form from the dropdown.
  4. Use the style controls to customize fields, labels, buttons, spacing, colors, and typography.
  5. Save and preview the page. The styled form appears where you placed the element.

You need Contact Form 7 and Bricks Builder installed and active.
