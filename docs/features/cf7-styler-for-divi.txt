Divi Styler Module
==================

What it is
  The main module that lets you place and style Contact Form 7 forms inside the Divi Builder.

How to use
  1. Edit a page with Divi Builder.
  2. Add the "CF7 Styler" (or "CF7 Mate") module from the Divi module list.
  3. In the module settings, choose your Contact Form 7 form from the dropdown.
  4. Use the Design and Content tabs to change layout, spacing, colors, and field styles.
  5. Save and view the page. The form appears where you placed the module.

You need Contact Form 7 and Divi Theme installed and active.
